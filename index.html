About

Dr. Sneha Das is a highly qualified Consultant Clinical Psychologist with over 10 years of experience in clinical practice, research, and teaching. She completed her M.Sc. and M.Phil. from the Institute of Psychiatry (IOP), Kolkata — one of India's foremost institutions for mental health training — and her Ph.D. in Clinical Psychology from the Central Institute of Psychiatry (CIP), Ranchi. She is an ex-faculty and practitioner at INHS Asvini, a prestigious naval hospital, and has authored five peer-reviewed publications. Dr. Sneha speaks English, Hindi, and Bengali, making her accessible to a wide range of patients.

About

Dr. Sampurna Chakraborty is an accomplished Consultant Clinical Psychologist with over 9 years of clinical and research experience. A Gold Medalist from the Institute of Psychiatry (IOP), Kolkata, she completed her doctoral research at the Central Institute of Psychiatry (CIP), Ranchi. She has served as a Research Associate at NIMHANS, Bengaluru — India's premier neuroscience and mental health research institution — bringing a strong evidence-based grounding to her clinical work. With 12 peer-reviewed publications, 3 awards, and a certification in Clinical Psychology, Dr. Sampurna brings exceptional academic rigour to her patient care. She speaks English, Hindi, and Bengali.
